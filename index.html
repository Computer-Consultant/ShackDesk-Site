ShackDesk

Windows desktop utilities for amateur radio operators. Built offline-first, for the shack and the field.

Download PortPane About the Project
The Suite
PortPane
"Takes the pain out of ports."

Audio device and COM port display and switching for digital mode operators. One click to load your profile and get on the air.

Learn more →
RigCheck In development
"Know your rig is ready."

Serial CAT communication diagnostics using Hamlib. Verify your radio connection before it matters.

MarkMySpot
"Your location, in ham-friendly formats."

Maidenhead grid square, lat/lon, and DMS coordinates — instantly on any device. Installable PWA, no account required.

Learn more →
Design Philosophy

ShackDesk apps are offline-first. No internet connection required, no telemetry without your consent, no nagware. Designed to work in the field, at a club station, or on a go-kit laptop just as well as at your home shack.

Source code is publicly available on GitHub. Contributions welcome.
